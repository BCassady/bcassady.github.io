Professional Experience

Sophomore, Research Assitant, and Teaching Assitant at Rice University

Current Work

I am currently working in the Unhelkar Robotics Lab at Rice University under Dr. Vaibhav Unhelkar. Dr. Unhelkar's Lab focuses on artificial intelligence and human-computer interaction. At the moment, I am using ROS (Robot Operating System) to develop primitive actions on the Stretch RE1 Robot. These actions will be implemented into a POMDP (partially observable Markov decision process) algorithm to allow the robot to navigate throughout a course collecting desirable objects. Additionally, these actions will be built into a library for other students in the lab to use.

I am also working as a Teaching Assistant for COMP 140 Computational Thinking at Rice University. I hold weekly office hours, grade assignments and tests, and hold one-on-one meetings with students. I also am planning an event for all of the COMP 140 students (more details to come, currently a secret).
